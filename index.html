Regal Disclaimer Software Development Services - "RD SDS"

RD SDS is more than a codeshop - we are software architects and engineers who deliver a rock-solid and efficient business solution using a suite of modern software development technologies.

Traditional software development services offered by our competitors are made to require updates and troubleshooting. Their project workflow rely on a client-generated specification, to which poorly tested makeshift software is added on until requirements are met. This often leads to unnecessary and inefficient code, as well as poor control over quality. The process resembles tacking on bits of clay until the 'pot' is able to hold all the contents - and will often have issues with structural integrity and require maintenance.

RD SDS offers something entirely different. Instead of going through the time consuming and expensive work of shaping clay around the contents, only to not be able to predict how strong the resulting structure is, RD SDS utilizes tested and industry proven tools to create a modular architecture, not only saving time and costs, but also requiring less maintenance.

With RD SDS, Each client project begins with a general survey of the client's past solutions, current needs and future goals. From this survey, multiple blueprints are generated with information on the costs and benefits of each solution set. These solutions not only meet the client's needs and goals in an efficient manner, but also often correct and improve previously installed parts of the architecture. The result is a robust software architecture plan that improves the client's overall software stack, rather than merely adding on a module that does not work well with the rest of the infrastructure.

After the client decides on a solution set that they are happy with, the software blueprint is turned into a software architecture built by tested and reliable tools. Thanks to our experience with modern tools, we are often able to offer our robust solutions at much lower price as well as faster time. Further, the disciplined architecture makes inspection and future upgrades inexpensive, manageable, and easy.
